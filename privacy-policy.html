Bunny Climb — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Bunny Climb is a paid casual game. We respect your privacy and do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Data Collection

Bunny Climb does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal details to play. We do not operate any servers that receive your data.

Game Data

High scores and game progress are stored locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to any server and is not accessible by us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Bunny Climb does not include any third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services. The game runs entirely offline once installed and does not communicate with any external services.

Children's Privacy

Bunny Climb is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our GitHub repository at github.com/TwilightAIMaster/bunny-climb.
